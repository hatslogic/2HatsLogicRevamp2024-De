Bike2Care - German e-bike online store

Client Location: Barsinghausen, Hanover, Germany

Bike2Care GmbH is an e-bike production company based near Hanover, Germany. What makes them unique is their vision on helping more people use electric bikes with more sustainable means of charging. The Pedelec from bike2care helps people to keep fit and reduce the burden on the environment. 

Bike2Care required a makeover for their online store to reflect the quality  and the purpose it stands for. Thats when we partnered with them in this project of migrating their existing Shop from Shopware 5 to Shopware 6.
