The problem

Designing a futuristic and timeless shop

Bike2Care care approached us when Shopware 5 end of life was near and a migration to Shopware 6 was a necessity. That also means an opportunity to update the outdated designs. The challenges we had here was to meet the client’s expectations and deliver a design that was way more modern and futuristic to match their product line. It should give a feel of quality, perfection and at the same time present the product information with utmost clarity. 

From a core technology perspective it didnt raise any challenges because of our experience of migrating and building multiple shops.
